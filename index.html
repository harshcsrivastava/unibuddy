About the Project

Unibuddy is a socializing web app specifically designed for our college students to connect with like-minded people. It will feature:

  • A mini calendar to showcase upcoming events.
  • A teammate search option for collaboration on events.
  • A discussion forum to talk about events and relieve stress.
  • Potential chatbot integration to support student well-being.

Our goal is to foster connections and help students manage stress through community support and event participation.
